About Us

Eliminating poverty and illiteracy from its root, the top NGO agency in India has come forward and helped the large sections of underprivileged people. Keeping this thought in mind WeCare Foundation has initiated its step for the same. We are doing this through our well-planned and comprehensive programs in healthcare, poverty, education, environment, livelihood.
At WeCare, we are working for the poor and marginalized people living in the slums and resettled them. Here, we are fully dedicated to the task of transforming these thousand lives from vulnerabilities.
